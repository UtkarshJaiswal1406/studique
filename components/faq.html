Frequently asked questions

What is Studique?

+

Studique is a platform designed to empower students by offering a community space where they can collaborate, learn, and grow. It focuses on providing resources, guidance, and opportunities tailored for student success.

Who can use Studique?

+

Studique is currently designed exclusively for SRM students. It offers resources and tools tailored to the SRM curriculum to help students excel in their academics

What resources does Studique provide for SRM students?

+

Studique provides access to course-specific resources like notes, question papers, interactive learning materials, and real-time progress tracking, all aligned with the SRM syllabus.

Will Studique expand to include students from other universities?

+

Currently, Studique focuses solely on SRM students. However, we plan to expand in the future to include students from other institutions. Stay tuned for updates!
